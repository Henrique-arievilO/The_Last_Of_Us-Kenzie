An American post-apocalyptic drama television series created by Craig Mazin and Neil Druckmann for HBO. Twenty years into a pandemic caused by a mass fungal infection, which forces its hosts to mutate into zombie-like creatures and collapses society.

The series follows Joel (Pedro Pascal), a smuggler tasked with escorting teenage Ellie (Bella Ramsey) across a post-apocalyptic America. Guest stars include Nico Parker as Joel's daughter creaturesSarah, Gabriel Luna as Joel's younger brother Tommy, Merle Dandridge as resistance leader Marlene and Anna Torv as Joel's smuggler partner Tess.

The Last of Us was filmed in Alberta from July 2021 to June 2022. It is HBO's first series based on a video game and is a joint production of Sony Pictures Television, PlayStation Productions, Naughty Dog, The Mighty Mint and Word Games.
